The status of world migration in 2010 where each circle is proportional in area to that country's emigrants (those who have left their country) or its immigrants (those who have settled in a new country).

Hover over a circle to show the origins/destinations of the migrants. The width of the arrow is proportional to the number of migrants.

This visualisation is built by Peter Cook using data from The World Bank. Built with Raphael, jQuery and Tim.
